About the Event

A one-day satellite meeting for building a scientific community around consciousness in Latin America.

This ASSC 2026 satellite event is designed to initiate the formation of a Latin American Society for the Scientific Study of Consciousness by connecting people and turning shared interest into coordinated action.

  • Bring together researchers, clinicians, and students interested in consciousness science across Latin America.
  • Review and discuss the results of a community-wide survey on interest, expectations, and willingness to participate.
  • Define initial working groups based on shared priorities and areas of collaboration.
  • Identify concrete next steps toward establishing the society.
  • Outline key dimensions of the future society, including structure, priorities, and activities.
